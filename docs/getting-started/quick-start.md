## Exporting from ChatGPT

Regardless of which method you choose, you may need to export your ChatGPT data:

1. Go to [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com)
2. Click your profile icon ‚Üí **Settings**
3. Navigate to **Data Controls**
4. Click **Export data**
5. Check your email for the download link

!!! info "Processing Time"
    Export processing usually takes a few minutes but can take up to an hour for accounts with many conversations.

## Importing to Kelivo

After converting your conversations:

1. Transfer the `.md` files to your device
2. Open [Kelivo](https://github.com/Chevey339/kelivo)
3. Use the import feature to add your conversations
4. Start chatting!
